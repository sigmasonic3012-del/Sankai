Airport Roles & Departments

Explore every career path available at Sankai Airlines. Each department has unique responsibilities and ranks to progress through.

In-Flight Department
🥤

Cabin Crew

Cabin Crew are the face of Sankai Airlines in the air. They welcome passengers, conduct safety demonstrations, serve refreshments, handle in-flight requests, and ensure passenger comfort from boarding to deboarding.

  • ✓ Greet and seat passengers
  • ✓ Perform safety demonstrations
  • ✓ Serve food, drinks, and snacks
  • ✓ Assist passengers with special needs
  • ✓ Communicate with the Flight Deck
Ground Operations
🚧

Ground Crew

Ground Crew keep the airport running on the tarmac. They guide aircraft in and out of gates, load/unload baggage, refuel planes, and coordinate with pilots and gate agents.

  • ✓ Marshal aircraft to gates
  • ✓ Load and unload luggage
  • ✓ Refuel and inspect aircraft
  • ✓ Perform pushback operations
  • ✓ Maintain ground safety
Terminal Services
🎫

Airport Staff

Airport Staff manage everything inside the terminal. They help passengers check in, verify tickets, operate security, run gates, and answer questions at information desks.

  • ✓ Operate check-in counters
  • ✓ Verify boarding passes at gates
  • ✓ Run security screenings
  • ✓ Provide customer service
  • ✓ Make boarding announcements
Flight Operations
✈️

Flight Deck

Flight Deck crew are responsible for flying the aircraft. They communicate with ATC, perform pre-flight checks, taxi, take off, cruise, land, and keep passengers safe throughout the journey.

  • ✓ Communicate with ATC
  • ✓ Complete pre-flight checks
  • ✓ Taxi, take off, and land safely
  • ✓ Coordinate with Cabin Crew
  • ✓ Follow realistic flight routes
Entry Level

Trainee / Junior

New staff members begin here. Learn the basics of your role, observe senior members, and complete simple tasks.

Standard

Crew / Agent / Officer

Once trained, you take on regular duties independently. You handle flights, serve passengers, or operate ground tasks.

Senior

Senior / Supervisor

Experienced members who lead shifts, train juniors, resolve issues, and make sure operations run smoothly.

Management

Manager / Captain / Head

Top leaders of each department. They handle recruitment, host flights, manage staff, and report to owners.

Become a Sankai Airlines Pilot

Take control of the aircraft and transport passengers safely across the skies.

Pilots are essential to Sankai Airlines. As a pilot, you operate our aircraft, follow real-world aviation procedures, communicate with ATC and crew, and provide passengers with a safe and enjoyable flight.

  1. Follow all ATC instructions during taxi, takeoff, cruise, approach, and landing.
  2. Complete full pre-flight checks before every departure.
  3. Maintain professional radio communication with cabin crew and ground crew.
  4. Fly assigned routes and approach procedures for realism.
  5. Always prioritize passenger and crew safety.
  6. Never abandon your aircraft, intentionally crash, or troll during flights.
  7. Report any issues to supervisors or management after the flight.
  8. Attend training sessions to advance through pilot ranks.
🛫

Takeoff Phase

Request taxi clearance, perform runway checks, line up, request takeoff clearance, and rotate smoothly.

☁️

Cruise Phase

Follow your flight route, maintain assigned altitude, and coordinate with cabin crew for passenger service.

🛬

Landing Phase

Request approach clearance, descend carefully, align with the runway, and execute a smooth touchdown.

🎓

Training & Ranks

Start as a Trainee Pilot, then progress to First Officer, Captain, and Senior Captain through experience and exams.

Discord Public Rules

Community rules to keep Sankai Airlines safe and enjoyable for everyone.

  1. Please do not send insulting or offensive messages to other members.
  2. Please do not share any inappropriate, NSFW, or disturbing content in the chat.
  3. Do not use any hacks, exploits, cheats, or glitches during flights or in the airport.
  4. Do not spam, flood the chat, or repeatedly ping staff members.
  5. Respect all staff members, administrators, and fellow community members.

Sankai Airlines Staff Handbook

Rules and expectations for all Sankai Airlines employees.

  1. Please join the server at least 10 minutes before the flight opens.
  2. Do not use offensive or insulting language toward passengers or staff.
  3. Do not insult, disrespect, or argue with any staff members or management.
  4. Do not misuse admin commands or tools under any circumstances.
  5. Always assist any passenger who needs help or has questions.
  6. Wear the correct uniform for your role and department during shifts.
  7. Stay in character during flights and follow roleplay procedures.
  8. Report rule-breakers, trolls, or exploiters to supervisors immediately.
  9. Follow the chain of command and respect department managers.
  10. Represent Sankai Airlines professionally at all times.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Sankai Airlines.

How do I join a flight?

Join our Discord server, check the flight schedule, and join the game when a flight opens. Make sure to buy a ticket if you want Premium Economy or Business Class.

How do I become staff?

Open an application ticket in our Discord server when applications are open. You can apply for Cabin Crew, Ground Crew, Airport Staff, or Pilot roles.

Can I be a pilot?

Yes! Pilot applications are open regularly. You must attend training and pass exams to advance through pilot ranks.

Do I need tickets to fly?

Economy Class is free. Premium Economy and Business Class tickets are optional but give you extra perks and comfort.
